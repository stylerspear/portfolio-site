The Project

As an avid book reader I spend quite a bit of money buying used books online. The books themselves are quite inexpensive, it is the combined shipping costs of the individual books that hurts the wallet. My idea was this: Curate a subscription box of used books and mail to the customer on a monthly or bi-monthly basis.

The Problem

The potential problem I see with such a service is book selection. Every reader is different. How do we ensure that the customer receives a book title that they find interesting and worth the monthly cost?

The Solution

To guarantee client satisfaction, the site will have a user profile where they list their preferred genres of books (Sci-Fi, Fantasy, Mystery, Biographies).

We will also have categories to choose from such as 'New York Times Bestsellers' or 'Classics', so if the customer doesn't want to pick a specific genre, they can branch out and receive titles within genres outside their regular choosing, but still ensure a great read!
